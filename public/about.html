Got 2 Go

As a mom of two beautiful little girls and a spouse of a handsome and enthusiastic traveler, I find myself wishing and looking for clean bathrooms when on the road, abroad, or just out on the town. As an adopted Texan, I have come to appreciate Bucee's awesome bathrooms and wish all establishments had the same philosophy, but the reality is different. Finding a clean bathroom is rare, but when you add the need for a diaper changing station or handicap accessible facilities, the odds get even smaller. The hope for the app is to make it easier for anyone and everyone to find a clean and safe place 2 go.
